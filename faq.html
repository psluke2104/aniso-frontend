Fabric & Care 4 questions
We use two fabric categories: premium pima cotton for our cotton sets (breathable, soft, skin-friendly) and charmeuse satin for our satin collection (lustrous, fluid, luxuriously smooth). All fabrics are OEKO-TEX certified.

For satin sets: hand wash in cold water using a mild detergent. Do not tumble dry — lay flat in shade to dry. Iron on low heat with a pressing cloth.

For cotton sets: machine wash on gentle cycle, cold water. Tumble dry low or air dry. Iron on medium heat if needed.

Our fabrics are selected for their durability. Pilling is minimal when washed correctly. Colours may gently soften with time, which adds to the lived-in luxury feel. Always wash inside-out and avoid harsh detergents to preserve the colour and texture.
Yes. All fabrics used by ANISO are OEKO-TEX Standard 100 certified — meaning they've been tested and verified to be free from harmful substances. They're safe for sensitive skin.
