The Brand

Born from the belief that
evenings deserve elegance.

ANISO began with a simple question: why should the hours at home be any less beautiful than the hours outside? We believe in nightwear that carries a woman through her softest moments — styled, intentional, and quietly luxurious.

Designed for the Gen Z woman who knows her own worth, our collections blend timeless silhouettes with premium fabrics and a palette that feels effortlessly refined.

Standard shipping is 4–7 business days. Express delivery (2–3 days) is available at checkout for select pincodes. Orders above ₹1,499 ship free.
We accept returns within 14 days of delivery. Items must be unused, unwashed, and in original packaging. Reach out to us at care@aniso.in to initiate your return.
Visit our Size Guide for detailed measurements. Our sets run true to size. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for a relaxed, lounge-ready fit.
