Size Guide

Find your perfect fit. Our sets are designed for a relaxed, effortless silhouette — not body-hugging, always comfortable.

Fit Note

All ANISO sets run true to size with a relaxed, comfortable fit. If you prefer a more fluid silhouette, we recommend sizing up by one size. If between two sizes, consider your preference — snugger or roomier.

How to Measure

Use a flexible measuring tape for the most accurate results. Measure directly on the body over light clothing.

01
Chest
Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape parallel to the ground and your arms relaxed at your sides.
02
Waist
Measure around your natural waistline — the narrowest part of your torso, usually about 2–3 cm above your navel.
03
Hip
Measure around the fullest part of your hips and seat, approximately 20 cm below your natural waist.
